MathSoc Executives Officers

President

Responsibilities

The President is the chief executive of the Society. They oversee all the affairs of the Society, represent the Society at official functions and public occasions and works with affiliated organizations on behalf of the Society.
They also work with the other executives and ensure things are running smoothly. They are also in charge of liaising with the many Mathsoc clubs.

 

Contact: president@mathsoc.uwaterloo.ca
